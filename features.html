Wordish Features

Puzzle out as many words as you can from the given letters. Play at your own pace, no pressure. No time limit. Some puzzles are long, some are short. A new puzzle comes along every day.

NO ADVERTS, NO DATA MINING

No ads, ever. You won't waste your time or cell data on ads. And we don’t collect any data.

WORD LIST

A word list of about 150,000 words curated by the dictionary already on your iPhone. Your choice of either UK or US English.

STATISTICS

For when you want them, statistics for win average, overall rank, and streaks.

BADGES

Share your latest Wizard badge :)

THEMES

Themes in light and dark modes.

CONTEXT ACTIONS

Look up definitions for missed words. Share missed or found words.

PUZZLE TIMER

We have a timer (in minutes, lol), which you can turn off.

PLAYABLE OFFLINE

Play on the tube, on an epic trip in another country, or deep in the backcountry—no cell reception needed.
